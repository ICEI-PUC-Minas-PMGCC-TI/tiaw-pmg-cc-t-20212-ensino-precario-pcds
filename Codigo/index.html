FOCUS

FOCUS is a web-educational project originally created as a part of the CV of Computer Science in PUCMinas. In this case, we target the youger students with learning difficulties in any school subject. With his own professor helping at any time regarding the exact problem the student have, he may have better results than studying without all the atention needed.
